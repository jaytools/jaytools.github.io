How to Play

1. Start the Game

Click "New Game" to shuffle the cards and begin. All cards will be face down, waiting for you to discover what's underneath.

2. Flip Cards

Click on any card to flip it over and reveal the symbol. Remember what you see â€“ you'll need it!

3. Find Matches

Click on a second card to flip it. If both cards show the same symbol, you've found a match! They'll stay face up.

4. Keep Going

Continue flipping cards to find all the matching pairs. The game ends when you've matched every card on the board.
