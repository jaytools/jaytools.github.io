How Much Weight Should You Gain During Pregnancy?

Why Track Weight Gain?

Proper weight gain during pregnancy supports your baby's development and your health. Too little or too much can lead to complications.

IOM Guidelines

Our calculator uses Institute of Medicine guidelines, which recommend weight gain based on your pre-pregnancy BMI category.

Twin Pregnancies

Twin pregnancies require higher weight gain recommendations to support the growth of both babies.

Weekly Tracking

Weight gain should be gradual and steady, with most gain occurring in the second and third trimesters.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this calculator medically accurate?

Yes, our calculator uses the official Institute of Medicine (IOM) guidelines for pregnancy weight gain recommendations.

What if I'm carrying twins?

Select "Twin Pregnancy" in the calculator for adjusted recommendations specific to multiple pregnancies.

Should I consult my doctor?

Always discuss your weight gain plan with your healthcare provider, especially if you have specific health conditions.
