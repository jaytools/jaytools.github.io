✅ FAQs

What is a good typing speed?

A good typing speed varies by context, but generally 40+ WPM is considered good for casual users, while 65+ WPM is professional level. The average typing speed is around 35-40 WPM. Our typing speed test paragraph helps you measure where you stand and provides goals for improvement.

How is WPM calculated in your typing test?

WPM (Words Per Minute) is calculated by dividing the total characters typed by 5 (average word length), then dividing by the time in minutes. Our live typing speed test provides real-time WPM calculation so you can see your progress instantly.

What's the difference between the test modes?

Normal Paragraph mode uses everyday text passages for general typing practice. Numbers Only mode focuses specifically on numeric keypad skills, while Mixed mode combines letters, numbers, and symbols for comprehensive typing speed test paragraph training.

How can I improve my typing speed?

Practice regularly with proper finger placement, focus on accuracy over speed initially, use all ten fingers, maintain good posture, and take regular breaks. Our typing speed test 10 minutes option is perfect for extended practice sessions.

Can I use this typing test on mobile devices?
