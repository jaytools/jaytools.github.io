A: CGPA stands for Cumulative Grade Point Average. It's a grading system used in educational institutions to evaluate a student's overall academic performance.

A: Many universities, employers and official forms require your academic performance to be presented as a percentage, even if your institution uses a CGPA system. This calculator helps you make that conversion accurately.
