Scholarship Details

Eligibility Criteria

  • Currently enrolled undergraduate or graduate student
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale
  • Demonstrated financial need
  • Strong academic performance
  • Commitment to educational goals

Prize Amount

₹5,000

Scholarship amount for Indian students: ₹5,000

Our scholarship program provides financial assistance to help cover:

  • Tuition fees
  • Educational materials
  • Living expenses
  • Academic resources

How to Apply

Simple 4-step application process:

  1. Complete the online application form
  2. Submit academic transcripts
  3. Write a personal essay (500 words)
  4. Provide two letters of recommendation

All applications reviewed within 2-3 weeks

Important Dates

Application Deadline:

December 31, 2025

Don't miss out - Apply early!

Application Opens: 18-09-2025

Results Announced: February 15, 2026

Award Disbursement: March 1, 2026

Application Process

1

Complete Application Form

Fill out the comprehensive application form below with accurate personal, academic, and financial information. Ensure all required fields are completed.

2

Submit Academic Documents

Upload your official academic transcripts, current enrollment verification, and any relevant certificates or awards. Documents must be in PDF format and clearly legible.

3

Write Personal Essay

Compose a 500-word essay describing your academic goals, career aspirations, and how this scholarship will impact your educational journey. Be authentic and specific.

4

Letters of Recommendation

Provide contact information for two academic or professional references who can speak to your character, academic ability, and potential for success.

Frequently Asked Questions

Students currently enrolled in undergraduate or graduate programs, maintaining a minimum GPA of 3.0, demonstrating financial need, and showing academic excellence are eligible to apply. We welcome applications from all fields of study and encourage diversity in our applicant pool.

The TaskProper Scholarship Program offers ₹5,000 in financial assistance to selected students. The amount is determined based on individual financial need and academic merit. Funds are typically distributed directly to the educational institution or can be provided to the student for educational expenses upon verification.

The application deadline for the TaskProper Scholarship Program is December 31, 2025. We encourage early submissions as applications are reviewed on a rolling basis. Results will be announced by February 15, 2026, and awards will be disbursed by March 1, 2026.

You can apply by completing the application form on this page, which includes personal information, academic records, a 500-word personal essay, and contact information for two references. You'll also need to upload your academic transcript and enrollment verification in PDF format.
