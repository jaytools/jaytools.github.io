Practical Solutions for Everyday Tasks

Look, we built these tools because we kept running into the same annoying problems. Maybe you'll find them useful too:

Fix Blurry Screen Text Once and For All

Tired of squinting at fuzzy text? Our DPI checker figures out your monitor's exact pixel density so you can adjust your display settings properly. Super helpful if you do any kind of design work, programming, or just want your eyes to stop hurting after long work sessions.

Stop Procrastinating with the Pomodoro Method

This technique actually works - 25 minutes of focused work, then a 5-minute break. Sounds simple, but it's surprisingly effective for tackling big projects or study sessions. Perfect when you're feeling overwhelmed or easily distracted.

Create Party Invites Without the Hassle

Who has time to learn complicated design software for a simple birthday invitation? Pick a template, add your details, and you're done. Great for graduations, baby showers, or any celebration where you want something nicer than a basic text message.

Test Your Typing Speed (Might Surprise You)

Fast typing isn't just for secretaries anymore - it's a genuine productivity skill. See how you stack up, find your weak spots, and get better. Especially useful if you're applying for remote work where typing speed actually matters.
