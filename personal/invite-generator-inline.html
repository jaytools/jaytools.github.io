Your Questions, Answered

How to make free invitations online?

Making free invitations online is super easy! Simply choose from our 50+ digital invitation templates, add your event details (date, time, location), customize colors and fonts, upload your own photos if desired, and download instantly. No signup required - start creating your free printable invites right now!

Can I download invites for free?

Yes! All our digital invites are completely free to download. You can save your custom invitations as PDF, PNG, or JPEG files without any watermarks, fees, or subscriptions. Perfect for both digital sharing and printing at home.

What formats are supported for download?

Our invitation maker supports three popular formats: PDF (best for printing), PNG (great for digital sharing with transparent backgrounds), and JPEG (perfect for social media and email). All formats are high-resolution and print-ready.

Can I create wedding invitations for free?

Absolutely! Our custom wedding invitations templates are completely free. Choose from elegant designs, add your wedding details, customize colors to match your theme, and download instantly. Perfect for save-the-dates, ceremony invites, and reception cards.

Are the invitation templates mobile-friendly?

Yes! Our digital invitation maker works perfectly on phones, tablets, and computers. You can create beautiful party invites online from any device, and all downloaded invitations display perfectly when shared via WhatsApp, email, or social media.

What's better - PNG or JPEG download?

It depends on what you're planning to do! PNG gives you crystal-clear quality and works great for social media posts. JPEG creates smaller file sizes that are perfect for emailing to guests or if you're planning to print them at home.

Will these invitations look good when printed?

Definitely! Our invitation maker creates high-resolution images that look fantastic both on screen and on paper. The 3:4 aspect ratio fits perfectly with standard photo printing sizes too.
