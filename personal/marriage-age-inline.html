Legal Marriage Age: Know the Law Before You Tie the Knot

Before planning your wedding, understanding the legal marriage age in your country is crucial. Marriage laws vary significantly across the globe, and knowing the minimum age for marriage helps you stay compliant and avoid legal complications.

Legal Marriage Age in India (2025 Update)

India recently updated its marriage age requirements to promote gender equality and ensure both partners are mature enough for this life-changing commitment:

Current Legal Age Requirements:

  • Male: 21 years (no change)
  • Female: 21 years (increased from 18 years in 2021)

This change under the Prohibition of Child Marriage (Amendment) Bill aims to empower women with better education and career opportunities before marriage. The legal marriageable age now matches for both genders, making India one of the few countries with uniform marriage eligibility age.

Why India Changed the Legal Age for Women

The government raised the minimum marriage age for women to:

  • Reduce maternal mortality rates
  • Allow more time for higher education
  • Improve career prospects and financial independence
  • Promote gender equality in marriage laws
  • Combat child marriage effectively

Court Marriage Age vs Traditional Marriage

When it comes to court marriage age, the legal requirements are typically the same as traditional ceremonies. However, court marriage offers some advantages:

Court Marriage Benefits:

  • Simpler documentation process
  • No religious restrictions
  • Faster legal recognition
  • Cost-effective option
  • Inter-faith marriages allowed

Required Documents:

  • Age proof (birth certificate, Aadhar card)
  • Address proof
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Affidavit of marriage
  • Witnesses (3 required in India)

Frequently Asked Questions About Legal Marriage Age

Can I get married before 21 in India?

No, as of 2021, both men and women must be at least 21 years old to legally marry in India. Marriages below this age are considered child marriages and are punishable under the Prohibition of Child Marriage Act.

What happens if I marry below legal age?

Marriage below the legal age is voidable and can be annulled. The male party (if 18+) can face imprisonment up to 2 years and/or fine. Parents/guardians arranging such marriages can also face legal consequences.

Is the legal marriage age same for all religions in India?

Yes, the legal marriage age of 21 years applies uniformly across all religions in India. However, personal laws may have additional ceremonies or customs beyond the legal registration.

Can I marry abroad if I'm below 21 but above 18?

If you're an Indian citizen, Indian law applies regardless of where you marry. Even if another country allows marriage at 18, you must still comply with Indian legal age requirements.

What is the best age to get married according to experts?

While legal age is 21, experts suggest 25-30 years is ideal. This allows time for education, career establishment, financial stability, and emotional maturity—key factors for successful marriages.
