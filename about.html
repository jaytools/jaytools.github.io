ABOUT Us

About Us

Task Proper began as a passion project by Jay Prajapati to address everyday online challenges. Originally a simple tool collection, it has grown into a platform offering free, fast, and mobile-friendly calculators and utilities for students, professionals, and everyday users.

Important: Task Proper is not a project management tool. Instead, it's a collection of simple, mobile-friendly calculators and web tools designed to help you handle everyday tasks easily.

Our Mission

We believe technology should simplify life, not complicate it. Our mission is to create and maintain a collection of web-based tools that are intuitive, accessible, and immediately useful for everyone – from students to professionals to everyday users.

Every tool we build focuses on three core principles:

  • Simplicity: Clean interfaces that anyone can use without a learning curve
  • Speed: Fast-loading tools that provide instant results
  • Accessibility: Designed for everyone, regardless of device or technical ability

We want anyone, anywhere, to access our tools and get the help they need in seconds, not minutes.
