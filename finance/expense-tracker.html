About the Daily Expense Tracker

This tool helps you easily track your daily spending habits. By logging your expenses, you can gain better insight into where your money goes, helping you manage your budget more effectively and achieve your financial goals.

Easy Expense Entry

Quickly add expenses with name, amount, category, and date.

Why You Should Start Tracking Your Daily Expenses

Ever wonder where your money disappears by the end of the week? You're not alone. Most people don't realize how much they spend on small things—like snacks, online deals, or daily coffees—until it's too late. That's why tracking your daily expenses really helps.


By writing down or logging what you spend each day, you can actually see your spending habits. It's like holding up a mirror to your wallet. You start noticing patterns—maybe you're overspending on food, subscriptions, or random stuff you don't need.


This small habit gives you better control over your money. It helps you avoid unnecessary purchases, stick to your monthly budget, and start saving for things that really matter—whether it's a trip, an emergency fund, or something big you've been planning.


You don't need to be a finance expert. Just start tracking. One step at a time. You'll be surprised how powerful this simple practice can be.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I add a new expense?

Just fill in the details like expense name, amount, category and date, then click on the Add Expense button. Your entry will instantly appear in the table below.

Can I remove an expense after adding it?

Yes, every expense you add includes a Delete button beside it. Simply click it to remove that item from your list.

Where is my expense data stored?

All your data is saved directly in your browser using local storage. That means it stays on your device and doesn't get uploaded or shared online. Even if you close the page, your entries will still be there when you return.

Can I download or export my data?

Currently, there isn't a built-in export feature. But you can manually copy the table, take a screenshot or print the page to save your expense history.
