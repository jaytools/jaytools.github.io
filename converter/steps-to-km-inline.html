How many kilometers is 5,000 steps?

How many kilometers is 10,000 steps?
10,000 steps is approximately 7.6 kilometers (4.7 miles) for an average person. Taller individuals usually have longer step lengths, while shorter people have shorter steps.
How do I calculate my step length based on height?
Your step length can be estimated using: Step Length = Height × 0.43 for men and Height × 0.41 for women. For example, a 170cm tall man has ~73cm step length, while a 170cm woman has ~70cm. This ensures more accurate distance conversion.
How many calories do I burn per step?
On average, you burn about 0.04 calories per step. For example, 10,000 steps burn ~400 calories, depending on weight, pace, terrain, and metabolism. Heavier individuals burn slightly more.
